The Worldwide Web or The Internet As We Know It

<

At this time, the ways we connected with the world was by Telegram, Phones, and novelties that most households either didn't have or afford. Until 1991, when America Online was introduced to the world. AOL's marketing was genius, they gave you a free Floppy Disk to install the application that lets you surf online for more than a year. And of course, we were all hooked. Without America Online, maybe we wouldn't even have the internet. Thank your ancestors, always.

IBM Simon - The First Smartphone?

<

This might not be your typical Android Phone, or iPhone (iOS device) but it was surely one of a kind. For a while at least, before all the new tech coming out every year. The IBM Simon was truly ahead of it's future. Considered the first Smartphone, ever. The IBM Simon had a touchscreen, it was handheld. But of course, doesn't have crazy specs like we do today. It did have Software Applications, but nothing normal in our era. Like Social Media applications like Snapchat or Instagram of course. It had an address book, calendar, appointment scheduler, and a calculator. Which in this time, was very & very useful.
