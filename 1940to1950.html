Software Evolution - 1940s to 1950s

The 1940s to 1950s was a place that Computers were not accessible as they were today, the name "Personal Computer" wasn't widely recognized. And many homes didn't have a computer, most of the time during this era. The computer or technology wasn't needed. Or, so that's what we thought at this time.

Here's the "computers", and such technology used in this time.

The ENIAC computer was created in 1945, right in our time period. Made by J. Presper Eckert at the University of Pennsylvania to calculate artillery firing tables for the United States Army's Ballistic Research Labratory.

Despite most people thinking Microsoft Windows was the first Operating System, most of them don't know they are incorrect. This was? -- Well, it doesn't look like an OS. Doesn't have any applications or anything. Well, it turns out it was. This is the Electronic Delay Storage Automatic Calculator or, EDSAC for short. This was created in 1947.
